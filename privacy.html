Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Blog Bot collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use the bot on idkstudios.org's Discord server.

2. Information Collected

The bot collects messages and usernames from users in the blog channel on Discord. This data is used to update the blog page on idkstudios.org.

3. Usage of Data

The data collected by the bot (messages and usernames) is solely used to populate the blog page on idkstudios.org. It is not shared with other users or used for any other purposes.

4. Third-Party Sharing

Your data will only be shared with Amazon Web Services (AWS) for the purpose of storing and processing the data securely. Your data will not be sold, rented, or shared with any other third parties.

5. Security Measures

We implement security measures to protect your data. The bot's data is stored on a secure server to ensure it remains safe from unauthorized access.

6. Data Retention

Data collected by the bot will be retained for a period of six months. After this period, the data will be deleted unless explicitly requested otherwise by the user.

7. User Rights

Users have the right to request the deletion of their data. You can do so by contacting me directly via email, direct message, or phone call. All users in the blog channel have my contact details.

8. Contact Information

For any privacy-related inquiries, you can contact me directly at my email address (provided to users in the blog channel).

9. Effective Date

This Privacy Policy is effective as of December 3rd, 2024 and is subject to change.
